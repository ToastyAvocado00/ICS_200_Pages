About Japan

Japan is an island country in East Asia located in the northwest Pacific Ocean.Japan is famous for its diverse attractions, ranging from ancient traditions like onsen hot springs and kabuki baths, which have been around since the 6th and 16th centuries, to contemporary delights like all-night neon dance parties, anime, and sushi boat restaurants. And let's not forget the stunning scenery of places like Kitanomaru Park, where cherry blossom trees paint a beautiful landscape.

Geography

Japan is made up of four main islands: Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyushu, and it's surrounded by nearly 4,000 smaller islands! Its closest mainland neighbors include the Siberian region of Russia up north, and Korea and China down south.
